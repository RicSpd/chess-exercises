Enable to enter specific piece types and quantities. Otherwise, amounts and types of pieces on the board will be completely random, i.e. a player can get from 0 up to 31 pieces.

If selected, the board could have a maximum of 8 pieces per type of piece, e.g. 8 queens, 4 rooks, 2 bishops, 5 knights.
Otherwise, the maximum amount of each piece will be the same as the starting position: 8 pawns, 2 knights/bishops/rooks, 1 queen.

White and Black will have equal material on the board.
